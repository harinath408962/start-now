Stop Waiting for Motivation.

Start Now.

A procrastination-focused learning assistant
designed for students aged 18–24

Most students don’t fail because they lack resources. They fail because they **delay starting**.

This platform helps you overcome procrastination by focusing on small starts, awareness, and daily reflection — not pressure or unrealistic schedules.

1. Recognize your state

Are you bored, tired, stressed, or focused? Your state decides the next step.

2. Start small

No full plans. No overload. Just one small, doable action.

3. Reflect & improve

Understand why you delayed today, so tomorrow becomes easier.

Example Case

A student plans to study at 7 PM but keeps delaying.

Instead of forcing a long session, the system suggests:
“Start with 10 minutes.”

The student starts — and often continues.
